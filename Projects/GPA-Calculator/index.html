The GPA Calculator is exactly what it is: A calculator that calculates your GPA. It saves you time if you have a bunch of AP or IB classes, and you want to get your weighted GPA without having to add the multiplier to each AP or IB class.

Here's an example: You have 2 pre-AP classes and 3 AP classes. You want to calculate your weighted GPA, but that means applying the correct multiplier to each class (which takes time).

This tool is meant to save you time on calculations and get you your GPA quickly (both weighted and unweighted). The full source code is available on GitHub.
