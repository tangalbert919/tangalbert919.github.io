WARNING: This mod is for hardcore players. I am not responsible for emotional meltdowns, destruction of property, or any other emotional, mental, or physical damage to you and others. If you blame me for this, I will simply laugh at you, because I just warned you about this.

This is the Hardcore+ Generator, a mod that runs alongside the Vanilla world generator to make new worlds much more scary. It makes survival much harder, causing you to stay on your toes and actually keep moving to find ways to stay alive in such a horrific world.

Currently, this mod only generates flowing lava and TNT in the air in the normal dimension, and then only lava in other dimensions. More will be added to this world generator soon, so be sure to check back often.

As of April 17, the source code has been released to anyone who wants to contribute to this mod and make it better. Those who help will be in the credits.
