What is nds4droid?

nds4droid is a DS emulator made by Jefferey Quesnelle. It's one of the open-source emulators found on the Google Play Store, and obviously, it only works on Android devices. It can run whatever ROM you have, but its performance is determined by what device you have. The newer (and stronger) your device is, the better performance you will get.

Currently, version 47 is the official verison of nds4droid, and can be found on the Google Play Store. An unofficial version (v47.1) can be found on GitHub, and is supposed to bring better performance. Version 48 is currently in development, and can also be downloaded from GitHub. However, there will be bugs in the development builds, so proceed with caution.
